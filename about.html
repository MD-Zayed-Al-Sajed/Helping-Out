About EcoGeorgian

Our Mission

EcoGeorgian is a leading wildlife rescue and preservation organization dedicated to safeguarding animals from both oceanic and jungle environments. Our mission is to protect and rehabilitate injured and displaced wildlife, utilizing community donations to provide essential medical care and support.

Our Vision

We envision a world where all animals are treated with respect and compassion, and where wildlife thrives in healthy and safe environments. Our efforts focus on rescue, rehabilitation, and rehoming animals in need, while also promoting conservation and education initiatives.

Our Team

Our dedicated team of volunteers and staff work tirelessly to ensure the well-being of the animals we rescue. From providing medical care to organizing fundraising events, each member plays a crucial role in our mission to protect and preserve wildlife. Our Milestones

1000+ Rescues

Since our inception, we have successfully rescued over 1000 animals, providing them with the care and support they need to thrive.

500+ Rehomings

We have found loving forever homes for over 500 animals, ensuring they receive the love and care they deserve.

Community Impact

Our community outreach programs have reached thousands of people, raising awareness about wildlife conservation and sustainable practices.

Global Collaborations

We have partnered with numerous international organizations to promote conservation and protect endangered species globally.
